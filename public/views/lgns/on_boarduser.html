Automated Marketing

Would you like to turn on Automated Marketing?.

When your calls come in after business hours and go unanswered, PSX will capture the caller ID, add it to an outbound call campaign, and randomly make calls to the missed caller ID for 8 business days or until the caller opts out. You can always turn this option on later. Also, there are some more advanced options for routing and monetizing after hours calls in the IVR section.
