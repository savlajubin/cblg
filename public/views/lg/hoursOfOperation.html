Choose Between Marketing Automation or Designing a Custom After Hours IVR

If Marketing Automation is turned On, the application will capture phone numbers that call in after normal hours of operation and add the numbers to an outbound call campaign. Then, during the next business day, the system will automatically attempt to connect to the after hours phone numbers. When a person answers the automated call, he will hear a whisper that says "We're sorry that we missed your after hours phone call. Please hold while you are connected to a live representative.
