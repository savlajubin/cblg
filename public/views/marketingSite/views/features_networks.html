Features for Affiliate Networks


Manage Buyers: Onboard as many call buyers as you like with all of the usual perks of call software, for free.

Automatic Call Distribution (ACD): PSX simplifies complex call routing from your affiliates to your buyers.

Manage Affiliates: Use PSX to manage your affiliate program (calls and web leads) for free.

Pay Affiliates: Forget Stripe and high priced merchant accounts. Use PSX to pay affiliates every week for free.

Buyer Clusters: Build Buyer Clusters so that you can have affiliates ring to one number and route to many buyers based on complex conditions.

After Hours Callback Automation: Turning on this features tells PSX to capture the caller ID of missed calls, add the caller ID's to an outbound campaign, and get the caller back on the phone.
