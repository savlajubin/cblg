Features

Inbound

What’s up with the .io domain?
Little known fact -- .io domains were developed for use by countries in the Indian Ocean. Techies use it as an acronym for input/output. We were originally going to charge users based on throughput usage (input/output model). It's kind of like pay-per-usage hosting. But then we decided not to charge for hosting and the domain extension just tagged along for the ride.
