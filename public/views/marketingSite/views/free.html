What does PSX charge for?
PSX offers the following options for a fee: 1) plug and play usage of buyer clusters, 2) usage of its bank of vanity telephone numbers, 3) custom phone software development. These services are optional, meaning that a user can user the PSX software application in a fully functioning capacity without having to pay anything. Some may call it "freemium", but we don't believe that a few optional services even rise to the level of the "freemium" label. It's just free.
