Tracking Number: Users can advertise phone numbers on websites, in commercials, in emails, or where ever you want to track calls. Some users choose to point their toll-free numbers to their PSX Tracking Number. You may be able to save a few dollars by porting your toll-frees into PSX.

Ring-to Number: As the name suggests, this is the phone number that your Tracking Number will forward to. It is usually the final destination in a given line of "forwarding" numbers.
