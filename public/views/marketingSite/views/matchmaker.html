Matchmaker


PSX isn’t just some high-end call routing / call center / call gen network software. Our value prop runs deeper than software. We help users interact and thrive.

PSX employees are encouraged to introduce new affiliates and call centers to each other, when appropriate. We go out of our way to not only make sure that you have the best phone software in your toolbox, but also to leverage our relationships to your advantage.

Sometimes it’s just not appropriate to introduce affiliates to buyers and vice versa. For example, if a buyer brings an affiliate to PSX and would like to keep that affiliate exclusive to his offer, we will honor that. PSX keeps up with referrals and sub-user relationships, so we know when its safe to share contact info or make introductions and when it’s not.

> Create IVR’s to route calls based on a variety of business conditions
