Features for Advertisers and Call Centers


Automatic Call Distribution (ACD): PSX simplifies complex call routing. Use IVR's and Queues and intuitive ways.

Feed Top Salespeople: Use call routing to send calls to your best salespeople first, or however you see fit.

Priority Call Routing: Decide which calls get answered first and by who.

Time-Based Routing: Route calls to specific queues or phone numbers based on the time of day.

After Hours Callback Automation: Turning on this features tells PSX to capture the caller ID of missed calls, add the caller ID's to an outbound campaign, and get the caller back on the phone.

Cell Phone / Voicemail Routing: Run your business using only cell phones, softphones, VOIP phones, or a mixture of all 3. Agents can skip voicemail and route calls to their cell phones, if desired.

Amazing things that you can do for free with PSX:

> Send sales texts and emails

> Set up ACD to route calls to multiple buyers or internal queues

> Create IVR’s to route calls based on a variety of business conditions

> Get 500 (or more) 1-800 numbers for tracking, routing, and analyzing sales calls
